## Scoring methodology

Each tool is scored **1–10 across 7 criteria**, then weighted into a single **Overall Score (0–100)**:

| Criterion | Weight | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Practical usefulness | 25% | Does it solve a real problem? Most important. |
| Ease of use | 15% | Onboarding friction kills adoption. |
| Feature completeness | 15% | Depth and breadth of capability. |
| Automation level | 15% | How much does it do without you. |
| User satisfaction | 15% | Real-world poll signal. |
| Pricing | 10% | Is it priced fairly for what you get. |
| Update frequency | 5% | Active development matters but isn't decisive. |

**Value Score** = `Overall × 10 ÷ max(monthly_price, $5)` — capped at 100. Free, open-source, or bundled tools max out and earn a 🏆 trophy in the Notable column.

## Three sortable columns

- **Overall** — composite weighted score
- **Satisfaction** — user-poll signal (×10)
- **Value** — performance per dollar
